# Geoffrey Zheng

Hello there! I'm Geoffrey, and I'm a Ph.D. candidate in physics at the University of Chicago.

I enjoy thinking about **physics, math, history, sociology, anthropology, and linguistics**.

## Research

I work in the [DeMille group](https://demillegroup.psd.uchicago.edu/) in the Department of Physics at UChicago. We are a group of experimental atomic, molecular, and optical (AMO) physicists who are broadly interested in producing cold/ultracold molecules for applications in quantum simulation and precision measurements of fundamental physics.

## Education

I obtained my Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) in [Physics](https://phy.princeton.edu/) from [Princeton University](https://www.princeton.edu/), with a minor in [Engineering Physics](https://engineeringphysics.princeton.edu/).
